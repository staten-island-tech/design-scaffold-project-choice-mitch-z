Dolan School Of Buisness

The Dolan School of Buisness is an extremely valuable asset to any student at Fairfield even for those not in any buisness major tracks. Similar to the Kelley Center, the Dolan School of Buisness also hosts career events and workshops for students. These events are very good at helping someone build their brand. You can learn how to set up a linkedin profile and how to make a good resume as well as how to do good in interviews. These skills are extremely valuable to any major and specilization.
