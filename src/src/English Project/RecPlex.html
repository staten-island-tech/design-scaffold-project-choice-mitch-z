RecPlex

Fairfields RecPlex offers alot of valuable things for all students. Whether its through the indoor gym, running track or swimming pool, there is something for everyone. The Recplex also offers private workout rooms and a PC setup. Besides being a place where one can build on themselves physically, the RecPlex offers alot of social opportunites which can come from intramural sports.

Intramural Sports are a great way to throw yourself out there even if your not the most social person. A team environment helps everyone make friends and just overall gives you something to do in your spare time. There are multiple intramuralsports offered within the recplex itself. The sports include basketball, volleyball, badminton, and dodgeball. Overall the RecPlex is a great place to spend your time, whether it be working out by yourself or with a friend, or doing sports.
